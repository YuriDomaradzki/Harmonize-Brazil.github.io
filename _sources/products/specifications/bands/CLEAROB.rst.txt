.. _clearob:

CLEAROB
-------


BDC CLEAROB band indicates the number of clear observations (no cloud, cloud shadow, snow or No Data) during the composed interval, according to the composed images cloud mask. Considering a 16 days composed data cube, if the CLEAROB indicates the value 2 in a specific pixel, this means that during the 16 days composition period, two images presented clear observations.
