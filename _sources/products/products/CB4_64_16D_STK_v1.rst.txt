CB4_64_16D_STK_v1
-----------------


The CB4_64_16D_STK_v1 product contains AWFI surface reflectance at full spatial resolution (64m) and 16 days Temporal Compositing applyed over the CB4_64 Identity Data Cube. The following Table lists the CB4_64_16D_STK_v1 product characteristics:


+-----------+-------------+-----------+-------------+------------+------------------------+--------------------------+
| CB4_64_16D_STK_v1                                                                                                  |
+-----------+-------------+-----------+-------------+------------+------------------------+--------------------------+
| Band Name | Common Name | Data Type | Value Range | Fill Value | Spatial Resolution (m) | Temporal Composting Step |
+===========+=============+===========+=============+============+========================+==========================+
| blue      | Blue        | Int16     | 1 - 10000   | -9999      | 64                     | 16 days                  |
+-----------+-------------+-----------+-------------+------------+------------------------+--------------------------+
| green     | Green       | Int16     | 1 - 10000   | -9999      | 64                     | 16 days                  |
+-----------+-------------+-----------+-------------+------------+------------------------+--------------------------+
| red       | Red         | Int16     | 1 - 10000   | -9999      | 64                     | 16 days                  |
+-----------+-------------+-----------+-------------+------------+------------------------+--------------------------+
| nir       | NIR         | Int16     | 1 - 10000   | -9999      | 64                     | 16 days                  |
+-----------+-------------+-----------+-------------+------------+------------------------+--------------------------+
| evi       | EVI         | Int16     | 1 - 10000   | -9999      | 64                     | 16 days                  |
+-----------+-------------+-----------+-------------+------------+------------------------+--------------------------+
| ndvi      | NDVI        | Int16     | 1 - 10000   | -9999      | 64                     | 16 days                  |
