S2_L2A
------


The S2_L2A image collection product contains data from the image collection :doc:`S2_L1C <./S2_L1C>` processed to Surface Reflectance data at its native spatial resolution through *Sen2cor* :cite:`louis:2016`, version 2.8.0, converted to GeoTiff, instead of JPEG2000, and with a nodata value set to -9999.
