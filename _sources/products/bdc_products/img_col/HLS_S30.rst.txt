HLS_S30
-------


The HLS_S30 image collection product contains Harmonized (NBAR) Sentinel-2/MSI surface reflectance data from the Harmonized Landsat Sentinel (HLS) project version 1.4 (https://hls.gsfc.nasa.gov/).
