S2_L1C
------


The S2_L1C image collection product contains Sentinel-2A/MSI and Sentinel-2B/MSI Top of atmosphere data at its native spatial resolution. These images are the same that can be found at Copernicus SciHub image catalog under the Sentinel-2 L1C dataset (https://scihub.copernicus.eu/dhus/#/home).
