MOD13Q1
-------


The MOD13Q1 collection product contains TERRA/MODIS Surface Reflectance data at its native spatial resolution (250m). These images are the same that can be found at NASA's Earth Date and USGS's Earth Explorer image catalogs (https://search.earthdata.nasa.gov/search or https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/).
